Services

Because eyebrows are the frame of the face, we want you to look an feel good. That is the reason we are offering eyebrow design. Choose one of the next services or both and receive a special price! 1. Eyebrow threading, this is the process of removing eyebrow hair using a piece of cotton thread. Some of its benefits are: More precise shaping and the want you decide. It is more accurate than waxing. It helps clean up those tiny, barely perceptible hairs that often get left behind during a wax. It is also great for sensitive skin, the process is quick and effective, removes multiple hairs at once with no anticipation, so there is very little time for pain receptors or your brain to react between removals. It is also Long lasting results, best for thicker brows and no use of chemicals. 2. Eyebrow tinting is the process of applying semi-permanent dye to enhance, shape, darken, and define your brows. It will lasts between three to six weeks, more or less, it will depend on the way you take care of them. How to take care of an eyebrow tinting: Avoid touching them for the first 24 hours, This can cause the tint to smudge or transfer onto your skin. Keep Your Brows Dry. It is important to avoid getting your brows wet for at least 24 hours after your treatment. After this time, you can resume your normal skincare and cleansing routine, but be sure to pat your brows dry rather than rubbing them. Use a Gentle Cleanser. When cleansing your face, be sure to use a gentle cleanser that would not irritate your skin or strip away the tint. Avoid using products that contain exfoliating ingredients, such as alpha-hydroxy acids, for the first few days after your treatment. Apply Sunscreen to o protect your skin and keep your brow tint looking its best. Look for a broad-spectrum formula with an SPF of 30 or higher. Avoid Using Oil-Based Products, such as makeup removers or heavy creams, can cause the tint to fade or run. If possible, avoid using these products on or near your brows, or use a gentler alternative.
